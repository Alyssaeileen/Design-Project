Why I Decided to do This Project
As a twin I get asked a lot of questions. For instance, is twin telepathy real and can you feel each theirs pain? Since I really did not know the answers to these questions I thought it would be interesitng to find out.

Common Questions Twins May Get Asked
As a twin I get asked alot of basic questions like who is older, do you dress the same, do you share a room, how can I tell you two apart, do you like the same things, what kind of twins are you, how much time are you apart? Those questions are easy to answer, but when someone asks you if twin telempathy is real, can you feel each others pain, why are you twins, or if it's rare to be a twin, it's more difficult to answer. That's why I'm writting a website about twin facts.
