Popular Twin Myths

Twins Skip Generations
There is no actual scientific proof that this is true but it is very common.


Identical Twins Have One Placenta and Fraternal Have Two
Typically this is true but it’s not always the case.


Identical Twins Can be Boy and Girl
Identical twins is when the egg splits in 2 so this is impossible.


Twin Telepathy is Real
Twins often have a special bond that differes from other siblings. They grow in the same environment at the same time.


Identical Twins have Identical Fingerprints
Even though they originate from one egg they still have different fingerprints.


Twin Pregnancies Always Results in Premature Babies
It occurs often but not always.


Having Twins Means Having a C-section
Only 60% percent of twin mothers have a C-section.


Having Twins Causes More Birth Complications
Just like having any baby there can be complications but it doesn’t mean that there will be. However, there is usually extra medical staff in the delivery room.
